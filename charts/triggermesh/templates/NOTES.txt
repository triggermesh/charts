*************************************************
*** PLEASE BE PATIENT: DEPLOYMENT IN PROGRESS ***
*************************************************

By default the `triggermesh-controller` can only manage resources only in the `{{ .Release.Namespace }}`
namespace. To allow the controller access to other namespaces, execute the following command in that namespace:

  kubectl create rolebinding {{ template "triggermesh.controller.fullname" . }} --clusterrole=triggermesh-controller --serviceaccount={{ .Release.Namespace }}:{{ include "triggermesh.controller.serviceAccountName" . }}
